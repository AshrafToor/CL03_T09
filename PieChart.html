Main Cancer Types

This chart illustrates the types of cancer causing deaths among men and women for the years 2017, 2019, and 2021. Each pie chart is divided by gender, showcasing the distribution of cancer types such as lung, breast, colorectal, liver, and others. The color-coded sections represent different cancer types, allowing for a clear comparison between genders. Lung cancer appears to be a significant cause of death for both men and women, while breast cancer is more prevalent in women.
