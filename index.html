Unveiling Cancer Mortality Trends: A Data-Driven Approach to Improving Health Outcomes




This website gives important information about cancer mortality in OECD nations. There are four primary aspects to our platform. The first aspect displays gender-specific cancer death rates for 2017 and 2021. The second aspect of this website shows expected cancer death rates in coming years till 2050. The third aspect covers the main cancer types by gender in 2017, 2019 and 2021. The strategies for preventing cancer are also provided. Our mission is to provide easily available and useful information to the users.
